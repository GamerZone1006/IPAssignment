Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions to set a new password.

How do I transfer funds?

You can transfer funds via the "Transfer" section in your account dashboard. Simply enter the recipient's details and the amount.

What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?

If your card is lost or stolen, please contact our support team immediately to block your card.
